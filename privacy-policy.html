This Privacy Policy explains how Vivani ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website and our presence on platforms such as Pinterest and Instagram.

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name or email address unless you voluntarily contact us. However, our website and third-party services we use may automatically collect certain technical information, such as:

We and our service providers may use cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use our website, to improve usability, and to measure the performance of our content and affiliate links. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function as intended if cookies are disabled.

When you follow an affiliate link, you are taken to a third-party website (such as an online retailer), whose own terms and privacy policies will apply.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites and recommend that you review their policies before providing any personal information.

We may use the information we collect to:

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

5. Data Sharing

We may share non-identifiable, aggregated information with service providers or partners who help us operate our website, such as analytics providers or hosting providers. We may also disclose information if required to do so by law or in order to protect our rights, property, or safety.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our content is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can remove it.
